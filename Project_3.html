Modified hypoplastic model with fabric anisotropy

Â¿What did I do?

In this project I implemented a modified version of the model proposed by Liao et al (2022) . Which used a different density function and two additional components as state variables, one that captures the behavior of high dilantacy sands, and one that captures the effect of accumulated shear strain due to cyclic deformation εacc , this factor is previously discussed in Fuentes et al (2018) . Results produced by the model are highly accurate (MAPE < 7%) for drained cases. Under undrained conditions, some considerations are proposed.

A brief conclusion

In this project I developed a new constitutive model by merging different components to reproduce the behaviour of sands. Under drained conditions, the model captures with a better accuracy the dilatant behavior by introducing a new state variable. For undrained cases, a state variable is introduced as a modification of the one proposed by Fuentes et al (2018) to reproduce cyclic mobility effects.
